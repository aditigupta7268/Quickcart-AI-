How It Works

Three steps to a queue-free store

No hardware overhaul. No retraining staff. Just install QuickCart AI and watch your store transform.

01
📱
Customer scans QR code
Each product shelf has a unique QR code. Customers open the QuickCart app, scan the QR, and enter their desired quantity. The item appears instantly in their digital cart.
02
💳
Pay instantly — UPI or card
When done shopping, the customer pays directly from the app. UPI, debit/credit cards, and wallets are all supported. AI verifies the transaction in milliseconds.
03
🚪

A verified digital receipt is generated instantly. The customer shows it at the exit gate — no waiting, no billing counter, no queues. The future of retail, today.

Customer Experience

Sixty seconds from scan to exit.

No dedicated app download needed. Works in any browser. Scan the QR on any shelf label and the product is in the cart instantly.

01
Point at any shelf QR code
Each product has a unique shelf QR. The scanner decodes it in under 200ms and identifies the exact SKU, brand, and price.
02
